My name is Eline and I am a web developer based in San Francisco, CA. Most of my experience is with Ruby (on Rails) and Javascript but I am always looking expand into new languages and technologies.

Before making a switch to software development I worked in advanced quantitative market research for big Tech companies. In this role I built simulation models to predict consumer behavior. My goal is to leverage my consumer insights experience with my software development skills to drive impact at a fast-growing company.

On a personal level I have a passion for fashion and in my free time I enjoy drawing fashion illustrations (see on the right) and designing clothes.
